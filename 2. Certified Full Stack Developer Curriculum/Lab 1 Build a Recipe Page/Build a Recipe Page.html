Spicy Thai Peanut Noodles

A quick and flavorful vegetarian dish perfect for a weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound rice noodles (pad thai noodles or similar)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chopped broccoli florets
  • 1 (15-ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha (or more to taste)
  • Chopped peanuts, for garnish (optional)
  • Cilantro, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add bell pepper and onion, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and broccoli, and cook for an additional minute.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes, peanut butter, soy sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, and sriracha. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, until slightly thickened.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the sauce and toss to coat. Heat through for another minute or two.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with chopped peanuts and cilantro (optional).
